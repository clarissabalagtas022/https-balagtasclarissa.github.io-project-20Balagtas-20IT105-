About me

let's all know me

I am Clarissa Balagtas, a 22-year-old woman residing in Ubas, Barangay Gubat, Camarines Norte. My life, like the vibrant landscape of my hometown, is a tapestry woven with threads of family, community, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge. Currently, I am enrolled at Camarines Norte State College, diligently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computing Multimedia Studies. This path, though challenging, is one I embrace with unwavering determination, fueled by a deep-seated passion for technology and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the world.

My upbringing in Ubas has instilled in me a strong sense of community and resilience. The close-knit nature of our barangay has fostered a supportive environment where neighbors become family, and shared experiences forge lasting bonds. Growing up, I witnessed firsthand the hard work and dedication of my parents, who instilled in me the importance of education and perseverance. Their unwavering belief in my potential has been my guiding star, propelling me forward even during moments of doubt and adversity.

The decision to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computing Multimedia Studies was not a spontaneous one. It stemmed from a fascination with technology that began in my early years. I remember spending countless hours captivated by the power of computers, exploring their capabilities, and marveling at their ability to connect people across vast distances. This fascination evolved into a passion, a desire to understand the intricate workings of technology and to harness its potential for creative expression and problem-solving.
